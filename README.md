## Who I Am

I'm a **full-stack developer with a strong backend focus**, currently building large-scale business management applications at a product company. With ~1 year of hands-on production experience, I specialize in **distributed backend systems** — microservices, event-driven architecture, and the hard problems that come with making them reliable at scale.
